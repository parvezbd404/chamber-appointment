# Chamber Appointment System 2.1.6

- Reduces OTP onboarding to verified mobile, full name, age, and gender.
- Sends newly registered patients directly to appointment booking by default.

- Keeps email, blood group, city, and address optional for booking.
- Preserves the quick family-member workflow during appointment booking.
- Adds Bangla translations and mobile-friendly reminder styling.
